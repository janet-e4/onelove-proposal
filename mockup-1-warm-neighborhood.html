Our Story

Your Neighborhood Groomer, Not a Chain

Since 2013, One Love Pet Salon has been the go-to groomer for Southwest Las Vegas families who want something the big-box stores can't offer: a calm, unhurried experience where the staff actually knows your pet by name.

We're a family-run salon near Tropicana & Hualapai. Our team of six experienced stylists grooms all breeds — from anxious rescues to senior dogs who need extra patience — using oatmeal shampoo and a whole lot of care.

Customers stay with us for years. Some have been coming for over a decade. That kind of loyalty doesn't come from a discount — it comes from trust.
